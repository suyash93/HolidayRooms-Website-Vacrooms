The Vacroom Story

With the idealogy of helping people get homes in foreign lands, Vacroom was started as a mere trial in one city by a team of budding entrepreneurs.Today, Vacroom has reached to approx 7 major cities in California with more than 100 vacrooms acroos 7 cities with thousands of users looking forward to book a vacroom daily..

Vacroom makes the experience of an individual worth by giving the user  opportunity to create, use and transform the place as they want to, With Vacrooms available in various dimensions, its definitely a new experience for people as they spend their limited time at a new place.

Vacrooms started by a team of 5 international graduate students of San Jose State University with the motive of being able to easily avail renting options in an unknown place, has now being transformed into quite a vibrant company with around 75 employees working day & night just to bring the best renting services to our users in the best available price.

The USP of Vacrooms is that it provides total freedom to the guest to modify the apartment as they wish to and make it as their home for those many particular days.

With the company gaining momentum in couple of months, It can be very rightly said that brighter days are yet to come.
